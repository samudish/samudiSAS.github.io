About

Welcome to Manekya by Sas, where innovation meets tradition in the realm of fashion. I am Samudi Hewanayake, the passionate designer behind this brand. My creations are inspired by the mesmerizing St. Basil's Cathedral, weaving its vibrant hues and intricate architecture into a collection that exudes beauty and grandeur. Manekya by Sas is a testament to professionalism, attention to detail, and a commitment to sustainability and inclusivity. This fusion extends to our designs, featuring captivating tie-dye, intricate macramé crafts, and unique fabric printings. Explore a curated selection that celebrates individuality, inviting you to join us on a creative journey where fashion transcends boundaries. Thank you for embracing the distinctive allure of Manekya by Sas.
