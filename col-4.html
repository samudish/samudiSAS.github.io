BlueBreeze Set

Bootcut Jean with a Cropped Knot Top

  • Description

Introducing the "Blue Breeze Set," where the essence of a serene breeze is captured in every detail. The cropped top, skillfully tie-dyed in light and dark blue hues, exudes a carefree charm with a front tie detail. Complemented by printed sleeves, this ensemble pairs seamlessly with a classic white bootcut jean, creating a refreshing and effortlessly chic look that resonates with the modern fusion of St. Basil's Cathedral inspiration.

Use of Cotton, Voil and Twil
