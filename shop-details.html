About St.Basil's Cathedral

Situated in the center of Moscow, St. Basil's Cathedral is a historic masterpiece that skillfully combines architectural majesty with a vivid aesthetic that has unexpectedly become a fashion world muse. The famous onion domes of the church, which are embellished with a rainbow of hues and elaborate designs, are a reflection of the audacious and expressive style of modern clothing. Designers are inspired by the cathedral's timeless charm, interpreting its distinctive qualities into apparel lines that evoke its illustrious past while incorporating a contemporary aesthetic. St. Basil's Cathedral is a live example of how style and architecture can coexist, with each dome telling a tale that is consistent with timeless design principles.

About the Designer

Welcome to Manekya by Sas, where fashion meets artistry in a celebration of cultural fusion and innovation. Inspired by the breathtaking St. Basil's Cathedral, our designs seamlessly blend tradition with contemporary flair. Our collection showcases the intricate beauty of tie-dye, the timeless charm of macramé crafts, and the allure of unique fabric printings. At Manekya by Sas, we strive to redefine fashion by marrying heritage with modern aesthetics. Explore a curated selection of outfits that reflect the spirit of St. Basil's Cathedral, exuding a harmonious blend of sophistication and creativity. Embrace the allure of our distinctive creations and embark on a journey where style meets tradition.
